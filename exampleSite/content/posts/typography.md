The theme keeps a comfortable reading measure and a calm vertical rhythm.

## Headings breathe

Generous spacing above section headings keeps long reads scannable.

- Lists are simple and legible
- One accent color, used sparingly
- No shadows, no gradients

Inline `code` and links are distinguishable without relying on color alone.
